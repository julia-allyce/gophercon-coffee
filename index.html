What?

Join us for a low-key alternative to the GopherCon pubcrawl and enjoy seeing what downtown Denver has to offer.

We will be hosting informal meetups at 3 local coffee shops all within walking distance of the Marriott Hotel. Gophers are invited to stop by for a coffee, and have the chance to chat with other attendees.

Each selected coffee shop is in a different neighborhood of Denver that features numerous restaurants and bars. We hope you will enjoy a cup or two of joe and take to exploring the city for dinner or whatever.

The map also features local restaurants recommendations. These are just a handful of restaurants in each area. Exploration is encouraged!
